Getting Started with text-generation-webui

Text-generation-webui (known also as Oooba, after its creator, Ooobabooga) is a web UI for running LLMs locally. It's one of the major pieces of open source software used by AI hobbyists and professionals alike. This tutorial will teach you how to deploy a local text-generation-webui installation on your own computer, as well as walk you through the necessary steps needed to start making use of its capabilities.
